How To Get There

From the Airport

  1. After getting off your flight and collecting your luggage, purchase travel pass from the automated kiosks available at the Airport.
  2. Take the T2 tram for 12 stops (approx. 20 mins).
  3. Get off at the Unita station, where you will be greeted by the Basilica of Santa Maria Novella.

From the road

  1. Rent a car from one of the many vendors available in Rome.
  2. Drive up North for 3 to 4 hours.
  3. You will arrive at your destination.
